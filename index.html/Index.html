The Visionary Behind the Lens

Otilio J. Nuñez is a PADI Master Scuba Diver Trainer and multimedia author whose journey began in engineering and finance. A single dive ignited a mission to dedicate his craft to the ocean, merging cinematic artistry with scientific rigor to tell the ocean’s most urgent stories.

“The silence of a dying reef is louder than its screams. My life’s work is to ensure we hear it—and act.”

Ocean Voice is more than a film project—we run conservation campaigns, educational outreach, and direct action to protect marine ecosystems worldwide.

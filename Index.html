Our Structure for Impact

To effectively address the ocean crisis, we operate a two-pronged strategy under our parent organization, Ocean Guardian Life Foundation™.

Ocean Voice: The Media Arm

This is our global awareness engine. Through cinematic filmmaking like our series 'Thirteen Moons', we tell compelling stories that educate the public and inspire action. Our media activities are fiscally sponsored by the 501(c)(3) organization From the Heart Productions, Inc.

Project Guardian™: The Science Arm

This is our direct-action field unit. Project Guardian™ focuses on scientific data collection and coral restoration in Thailand and the Caribbean. A fiscal sponsorship agreement with the 501(c)(3) organization Ecologistics, Inc. to support these activities is in its final stages.

Thirteen Moons: The Reef’s Last Voice

This is not a story of despair. It is an invitation. With every frame, Thirteen Moons reveals both the beauty we risk losing and the solutions within our grasp.

Partner With Us: A Grant-Ready Initiative

Your support funds a scalable conservation model designed for global reach and tangible results in marine preservation.

A Silent Emergency

When ocean temperatures rise, corals expel the algae that give them life, leading to mass bleaching. This is a reef starving in plain sight, a crisis our action initiatives are designed to fight.

The Visionary Behind the Lens

Otilio J. Nuñez is a PADI Master Scuba Diver Trainer and the founder of Ocean Guardian Life Foundation™. His journey began in engineering and finance, but a single dive ignited a mission to merge cinematic artistry with scientific rigor to tell the ocean’s most urgent stories. He also leads world-class diving expeditions in Thailand through his booking portal, Buceo Koh Samui.

“The silence of a dying reef is louder than its screams. My life’s work is to ensure we hear it—and act.”
